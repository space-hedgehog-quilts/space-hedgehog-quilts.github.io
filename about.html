About

It started with a white dog who sheds and a dark grey couch. My mom wanted a throw blanket to protect the couch. I had a sewing machine so I decided to make a quilt. That was almost 10 years ago. Fast forward to today with a few brief breaks in between and I have made 6 quilts in 2021 so far!

I love taking tiny pieces of fabric and turning them into a beautiful quilt. I have tried my hand at designing my own blocks and I hope to have more chances to do that in the future. My 2 dogs try their best to help me, but they are seriously hampered by their lack of thumbs.
